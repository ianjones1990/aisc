How it all began...

Northern Ireland Global Starcraft, NIGS, was founded in 2011 by members Patrick "CluEleSs"McClurg and Stewart "Wombat" Millar. Together, this powerhouse duo would travel the island looking for the strongest talent and the most handsome men to join the prestigious ranks of All Ireland Starcraft's elite playerbase. After hosting the first official NIGS event at Q-Con 2011 in Belfast, the celestial gods of NIGS, henceforth known as The Pantheon, changed the name to Northern Ireland Starcraft, or NiS.

The NiS Logo - circa 2011


Jonathan McShane took over as glorious leader in 2012 and built upon the bedrock laid before him to grow it into the UK and Ireland's premier Starcraft community. It is currently 2018, and NiS, now All Ireland Starcraft, or AISC, has no plans of slowing down. Our unique blend of rash comedy, our don't-give-a-fuck attitude and our trademark velvet smoking jacket/outrun blasting/blue-eyed soul consuming aesthetics will keep our events fresh and our community active.
